### ◼ Overview

Cylvor IT is a forward-thinking development firm specializing in the creation of high-performance web applications and immersive digital interfaces. 

We distinguish ourselves through a rigorous adherence to modern engineering standards—specifically utilizing **TypeScript** and **Next.js**—to deliver products that are robust, secure, and highly performant. Our mission is to transform static concepts into dynamic, responsive, and profitable digital assets for our clients.
